📒 A range of Web3 or AI topics to choose from


You can participate in the hackathon with your existing team and idea if you're already working on something, or you can team up with other participants to bring a new idea to life.


Apply under these two categories:

  • New Ventures Category : This category is designed for newly formed teams and innovative ideas that have emerged only during this Hackathon. Whether you have a groundbreaking idea or just started building your team, this category welcomes all new ventures focused on Web3 and AI topics.


  • On the Fast Track Category : The On the Fast Track category is designed for established teams and projects that are already making significant progress in the realms of Web3 and AI.  If you've been working on a solution for some time and have reached a certain level of development or validation, this category is perfect for you.
